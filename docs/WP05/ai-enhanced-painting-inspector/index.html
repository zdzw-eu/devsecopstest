    AI-Enhanced Painting Inspector

    General Description

    Typically, computer vision systems used for production quality control are fixed in position, and products pass through the cameras’ field of view on a conveyor belt. However, this approach is not feasible in cases where the objects to be inspected have very large dimensions, such as windmill towers. Moving these products through a factory presents a complex problem in itself, so the approach here relies on moving the inspection system to avoid moving the product as much as possible.

    The AI Enhanced Painting Inspector app, developed as part of the ZDZW project, is focused on this specific type of environment.

    Top Ten Functionalities

    1. AI Inference on the Edge

    Capability of performing AI inference in the edge device deployed at the factory.

    2. Semi-autonomous Inspection Platform

    Possibility of having a platform that can be guided through the factory to inspect large objects.

    3. Compatibility with any GeniCam compliant camera

    Capability of connecting with any Industrial camera which uses the GeniCam standard.

    4. Facility to deploy Artificial Vision models

    Facility of improving or adapting the computer vision model to the specific problem.

    5. Screen visualization of defects

    Ability to monitor the system while performing the inspection.

    6. Collect samples for fine-tuning the model

    The possibility of acquiring additional images once the AI model has bee deployed. Thanks to that, novel versions of the AI model could be developing in parallel.

    7. Flexible camera-illumination setup

    The ability of having adjustability of the camera-illumination setup.

    8. Provide high-speed and low latency quality feedback

    Having the possibility of receive real-time feedback for the operator of the region of the object inspected.

    9. Obtain metrics to evaluate the inference accuracy of the model

    Visualise and analyse statistics of the defects detected and the accuracy of the model.

    10. Industry standard communications (e.g., OPCUA)

    Capability to communicate with the inspection system through OPCUA.

    Architecture Diagram

    The high level Architecture diagram from the Sofware Specification document

    • Painting Inspector: AI Defect detection deploymente software.
    • Positioning platform: The hardware that position itself in a fixed position respect to the surface.
    • Edge Front-end: GUI that connects through OPC-UA to the edge device, to monitor the system and visualize a live-view.
    • Cameras: GeniCam complaint cameras.
    • Defect Database: Databases that stores the defective patchs detected by the system, with their positioning and image.
    • AI Training Module: The module used to train new AI models based on the defects images.
    • Cloud Front-End: The interface that shows some statistics of the defect database.
